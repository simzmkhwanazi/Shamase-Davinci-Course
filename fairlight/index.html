How certification works

  1. Work through the Study Guide — 7 modules with lesson notes and knowledge checks.
  2. Sit Exam 1 — Fundamentals (interface, tools, terminology). Pass at 90%.
  3. Sit Exam 2 — Workflow & Decisions (real-world scenarios). Pass at 90%.
  4. Pass both exams within 3 attempts each to unlock your printable certificate.
📸 BEFORE YOU BEGIN — CONFIRM YOU CAN TAKE A SCREENSHOT At the end of each exam you MUST screenshot your final results page and send it to your instructor. Failure to submit proof of completion may invalidate your certification.
